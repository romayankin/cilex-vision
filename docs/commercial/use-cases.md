# Cilex Vision Use Cases

This document shows how Cilex Vision translates platform capabilities into operational outcomes for common commercial environments.

## 1. Retail Analytics

### The problem

Retail teams need to understand how people move through stores, where congestion forms, and how long staff spend reviewing incidents or disputes after the fact.

### How Cilex Vision helps

- tracks people and vehicles in and around the site in real time
- records entry, exit, motion, and loitering events
- supports camera-by-camera search and timeline review
- generates clip evidence for operational review
- enables cross-camera investigation at a site when a shopper or vehicle moves between coverage areas

### Business benefits

- faster review of incidents, disputes, and safety events
- better visibility into dwell-time patterns around entrances, checkouts, and service desks
- reduced manual video review effort for store teams and security staff
- stronger consistency across multiple stores using the same operational workflows

## 2. Campus Security

### The problem

Campus and estate security teams often manage a large number of cameras, multiple buildings, and limited operator bandwidth. Manual handoff between cameras slows investigations.

### How Cilex Vision helps

- detects people, vehicles, and animals across the estate
- links the same subject across multiple cameras at a site
- highlights loitering, entry, exit, and motion-related events
- provides searchable clips and thumbnails for rapid evidence collection
- supports site health visibility and centralized operations across multiple sites

### Business benefits

- faster subject handoff between buildings, gates, and external areas
- quicker escalation of perimeter and loitering events
- improved situational awareness for distributed security teams
- reduced dependence on continuous manual wall monitoring

## 3. Traffic Monitoring

### The problem

Traffic operators need to identify stopped vehicles, monitor traffic flow, and retrieve evidence quickly when incidents occur. Manual review is slow, especially across many cameras or corridors.

### How Cilex Vision helps

- detects and tracks cars, trucks, buses, bicycles, motorcycles, and pedestrians
- identifies stopped vehicle behavior through event rules
- supports optional license plate recognition with searchable results
- creates clip evidence tied to operational events
- provides structured search across camera, time, object type, and state

### Business benefits

- faster incident review and response coordination
- more efficient evidence lookup for vehicle-related cases
- clearer visibility into activity patterns at intersections, gates, and loading zones
- improved productivity for transport or parking operations teams

## 4. Warehouse Management and Safety

### The problem

Warehouses and logistics yards need a practical way to monitor vehicle movement, staff activity, and zone compliance without increasing manual supervision costs.

### How Cilex Vision helps

- tracks people and vehicles across warehouse, yard, and loading areas
- detects stopped vehicles and prolonged presence in sensitive zones
- supports search and replay for safety investigations
- links movement across adjacent cameras to reconstruct journeys
- works with edge buffering for sites where WAN connectivity is inconsistent

### Business benefits

- faster reconstruction of safety events and near-misses
- stronger visibility into yard congestion and vehicle dwell time
- lower manual effort for routine security and operational review
- better consistency across remote or semi-connected facilities

## Positioning Guidance

Across these use cases, the commercial message stays consistent:

- existing cameras become more searchable and actionable
- operators review events and evidence instead of continuous video
- multi-camera and multi-site environments become easier to manage
- the platform supports both operational efficiency and security response outcomes
