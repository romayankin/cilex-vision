## Tier Positioning

### Basic

Designed for smaller single-site deployments that need core video search, operational visibility, and event review without advanced analytics layers.

### Pro

Designed for organizations that want richer investigations and stronger automation at a site, including color attributes, cross-camera linking, advanced events, and edge-resilient deployment.

### Enterprise

Designed for distributed estates that need multi-site management, LPR, advanced operational controls, formal disaster recovery posture, and centralized rollout governance.

## Commercial Notes

- LPR can be positioned as an enterprise-standard capability or a controlled add-on depending on sector and regional policy.
- Final camera limits depend on scene complexity, motion levels, and infrastructure sizing.
- Contact sales for final entitlement, deployment scope, and support packaging.
